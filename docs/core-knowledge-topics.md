4. Core Knowledge Preschool Sequence (Ages 3-5)
=========================================================================

Two copies exist on Internet Archive:
- "coreknowledgeseq0000unse" (2000 edition, 182 pages)
- "coreknowledgeseq0000unse_i7p4" (2000 edition, 182 pages)

BOTH are in the "printdisabled" / "inlibrary" collections with access restrictions.
The full text content is NOT accessible without a controlled digital lending login.
No description or table of contents is available in the metadata.

Based on the project's uli-brain-architecture.md and general CK documentation,
the Preschool Sequence covers these broad areas for ages 3-5:
- Oral Language
- Nursery Rhymes, Poems, and Fingerplays
- Storybook Reading and Storytelling
- Emerging Literacy Skills
- Math (Number Sense, Geometry, Patterns)
- Scientific Reasoning and the Physical World
- Music
- Visual Arts
- Movement and Coordination
- Autonomy and Social Skills

5. Core Knowledge Sequence K-8 (Master Document)
=========================================================================

Available on Internet Archive:
- "coreknowledgeseq0000unse_u1l5" (1999 edition, 232 pages, ISBN 1890517208)

This is also access-restricted ("printdisabled" collection).
Description: "The Core Knowledge Sequence is a detailed outline of specific content
to be taught in language arts, history, geography, mathematics, science, and the
fine arts. As the core of school's curriculum, it can provide a solid, coherent
foundation of learning, while allowing flexibility to meet local needs."

The 2023 edition is available for free download from coreknowledge.org via a
Gravity Forms submission (email required), but the PDFs are not directly linkable.

## Kindergarten Topics by Subject (from the 1999 Sequence)
Based on the CKLA K domains and CKHG units above, the Kindergarten Sequence covers:

### Language Arts
- Nursery Rhymes and Fables
- The Five Senses
- Stories (classic tales)
- Plants
- Farms
- Native Americans
- Kings and Queens
- Seasons and Weather
- Columbus and the Pilgrims
- Colonial Towns and Townspeople
- Taking Care of the Earth
- Presidents and American Symbols

### History and Geography
(Overlaps significantly with CKLA Listening & Learning domains above)
- Basic world geography (continents, oceans)
- Native American peoples
- Early exploration (Columbus)
- Colonists and pilgrims
- Presidents (Washington, Lincoln)
- American symbols (flag, Statue of Liberty)

### Science
- Plants and plant growth
- Seasons and weather
- The five senses
- Taking care of the Earth (conservation)

### Mathematics
- Counting and recognizing numbers to 20
- Basic addition and subtraction concepts
- Patterns, shapes, measurement
- Money recognition
- Time (calendar, days of week)

### Visual Arts
- Color recognition and mixing
- Lines, shapes, and textures
- Selected artworks from CK art cards

### Music
- Singing and rhythm
- Selected listening pieces

NOTES ON DATA GAPS
=========================================================================

1. CKLA PreK domains: NOT found. CKLA was originally designed for K-5.
   The PreK version is a separate publication and its domain titles
   are not available in any accessible source.

2. CKHG Kindergarten: NOT found as a standalone listing. The CKHG K
   program was added in the newer editions. The CKLA K domains serve
   as the history/geography content for Kindergarten.

3. CKSci Kindergarten: NOT found. CKSci is the newest CK program
   and its title lists are only accessible via the CK website's
   JavaScript-driven download page.

4. Core Knowledge Sequence (detailed topic outlines): The actual detailed
   topic lists within each subject area (e.g., specific history topics,
   specific science topics for each grade) are in the 218-page Sequence
   document, which is access-restricted on IA and downloadable from
   coreknowledge.org only via a form submission.

5. The coreknowledge.org website has been completely restructured.
   All old sub-page URLs return 404. The WordPress REST API is blocked
   by the Defender security plugin. The download-curriculum page uses
   JavaScript filtering that non-JS tools cannot access.
